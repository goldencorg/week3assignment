The Rebuild

Hello! I'm Nathan. This website is based on my career as Offensive Coordinator of the Cal Golden Bears in the Dynasty mode of the game College Football 25. My goal was to bring this 3.5 star program back to glory. I am currently in season 4, year 2027 of this career save, coming off a dominant run from the previous season. On this site, I have displayed my most important players on offense, while also including valuable pieces from the defensive side.


I bought CFB 25 out of curiosity, interested in how it would compare with Madden games. It led me to gain a much better understanding of college football and even become a big fan of it. Now I follow it every week and can't get enough of it, while slowly learning more about the strategical and analytical aspects of football.


While the Golden Bears will have my support in real life, due to being their OC in CFB 25, I will always support the USC Trojans above everyone else. Fight on!
